Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This honey chicken dish is easy to make with just 5 ingredients. I've used this recipe for years, and my familly requests it often. Delicious served on steamed rice and garnished with sliced green onions.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red peper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
